PLM Education Email and How to Use Them


PLM has given each faculties and students an email account in partnership with Microsoft. But it can do much more than just being able to use Microsoft products like, Outlook, OneDrive, or Office. Here are some softwares that the faculties and students can use for their work and studies by just signing up and logging in with their PLM email account.

Canva Education

Canva is an online graphic design tool to create social media posts, presentations, posters, videos, logos and more.

Coursera

Coursera is an education platform that partners with top universities and organizations worldwide, to offer courses online for anyone to take,

Autodesk

Autodesk is a design software company that serves customers across the manufacturing, architecture, building, construction, media, and entertainment industries.

Notion

Notion is a project management and note-taking software platform. It makes ollaborative work online easier.

Figma for Education

Figma is a design tool that lets you create designs for mobile and web interfaces, or any other kind of design you can think of.

GitHub Student

GitHub is a code hosting platform for version control and collaboration. It lets you and others work together on projects from anywhere.
